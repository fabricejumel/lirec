How to play

Click on seeds to pick them, and then in free space on the ground to plant them. Move around the world by clicking on the arrows. The plant spirits will tell you more about the world and the plants it contains. You can click on the agent's heads to see the FAtiMA debug information. The game is still under active development, so things will change unexpectedly!
