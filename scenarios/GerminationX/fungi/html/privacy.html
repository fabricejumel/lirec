Privacy Policy

Germination X stores no information about you on its servers, except the name you enter (and eventually it will store your public Facebook User ID number).

When accessing http://apps.facebook.com/germinationx a cookie is set by Facebook. The cookie is used to verify you are logged into Facebook. It expires as soon as you log off from Facebook. For details on Facebook's use of cookies: http://www.facebook.com/policy.php

When accessing the game via other urls, this cookie is not created.

Any information connected to you in the game is only fetched from Facebook and displayed to you locally, on your computer, and is not stored or shared with others.

While you play the game, your actions and any data you generate within the game will be visible to anyone playing and are stored on our server. We may use this for further studies of user behaviour.

You may at any time remove your data from our server, by sending "dave at fo dot am" an email (this option will be automated soon).
